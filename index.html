About Me

My name is Thanasis Angelis.

I am a Computer Science student at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens with a strong interest in software engineering and web development, particularly backend and full-stack systems. I enjoy building applications end-to-end, focusing on clean architecture, well-designed APIs, and reliable data models that reflect real-world use cases.

My experience is primarily centered around backend and full-stack web development, using Node.js (Express) and PostgreSQL to design and implement complete web applications. I am motivated by challenging problems that require thoughtful design decisions and trade-offs, and I continuously strive to deepen my existing knowledge while learning new technologies. I am also interested in cybersecurity, with a focus on building secure and dependable software systems.
